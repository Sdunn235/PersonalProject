# 🌍 World Simulation Design Codex (Draft)

## 1. Theme + Atmosphere
- **Magitek cyberpunk fantasy**: neon skylines, dystopian megacities, magic = computation, technology = ritualized sorcery.  
- **Tone**: like FF7/FF15 meets cyberpunk dystopia — sleek + broken, modern + mystical.  
- **Core truth**: bytes + atoms are the same substrate; manipulating the world = rewriting its “code.”

---

## 2. Stats (shared by players + AI)
- **VIT (Vitality)** → HP, stability vs corruption.  
- **STR (Strength)** → physical damage, presence in material layer.  
- **DEX (Dexterity)** → accuracy, evasion, initiative (sync with tick rate).  
- **MAG (Magic)** → spell damage, resonance with arcane substrate.  
- **WIS (Willpower)** → buffs/debuffs, resist influence, construct stability.  
- **LCK (Luck)** → crits, anomalies, probability-bending.  

*Lore fit:* NPCs and players see these as “The Six Pillars” of existence.

---

## 3. Mechanics = World Physics
- **Resonance Fields (MAG+WIS):** actions shift your resonance with the Arcane Layer; world zones amplify or disrupt.  
- **Anomalies (LCK):** bending probability → glitches, desyncs, rare events.  
- **Corruption (VIT+MAG):** entropy/data-rot; healing = stabilizing form.  
- **Tick Rate (DEX):** world sync, affects turn order and timing.

---

## 4. Resource World Model
- **Atomic resources**: dirt, wood, stone, water, ore, energy.  
- **Transformable recipes**: tree → lumber, ore → iron, sand → glass.  
- **Structures**: walls, tools, buildings.  
- **Systems**: farms, cities, trade.  
- **Culture/art**: monuments, decorations, sandcastles.  

*Each resource has*: type, quantity, state, location.  

---

## 5. Verbs (shared by all agents)
- Harvest (remove resource).  
- Refine (change state).  
- Construct (arrange into structure).  
- Alter (change environment).  
- Combine (merge for new result).  

---

## 6. AI Needs + Motivations
- **Survival**: hunger, shelter, safety.  
- **Production**: tools, wealth, trade.  
- **Art/Curiosity**: non-utility recombination (how sandcastles emerge).  
- **Shared parity**: if players can do it, AI can too.

---

## 7. Emergence Principles
- **Players and AI manipulate the same ruleset.**  
- **Curiosity triggers invention.** (low-pressure state → whimsical actions).  
- **Culture evolves**: one AI builds something odd → others copy → becomes “tradition.”
